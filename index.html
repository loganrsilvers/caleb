Hello, I'm Caleb McDermott.

I am a Chicago based Product photographer who has most of my experience in commercial and other studio work. I chose photography because I wanted to ensure that my memories can be captured and seen by all my loved ones, so why not share it with the world?

My approach differs based on the style of work, but I love candid photos more than anything. Capturing raw moments is what I excel at. My biggest passion besides photography is music, so the best thing I have learned to do is to find a new CD from a shop and try it out while editing! I have been doing photography for 5 years, but I hope to refine my skills as I grow. Contact me for any visions you have!
